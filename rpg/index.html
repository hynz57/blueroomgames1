Destiny

In case you have been living under a rock for the past year, Bungie, the developers of the Halo franchise, have announced their new title, Destiny. This thrilling game is an MMO RPG FPS like you have never seen! It takes place in the distant future on many planets throughout the solar system. It is a sci-fi done right thanks to the 55 million dolers pumped into this game. This game just ended its beta a few weeks ago and had over 50 millon participants! Thats freakin ridiculas! You will find yourself immersed in a Star Wars like universe mixed with Halo style lore and Borderlands rate adventure. This game is gonna be huge!

New Bethesda project?

With the last project done by Bethesda studios being released years ago, many are wondering when they will see a new title by the company. Most people believe that the developers will be taking a break from the Elder Scrolls games and going back to one of the most classic RPG games ever. Fallout. Many believe that Bethesda's next announcement will be that of Fallout 4. Gamers have already found much evidence supporting the next title in the Fallout seris to be based in Massechusets in the area known in the Fallout lore as the Commonwealth. This is just pure skepticism, but gamers are sure hoping to get back into that post-apocolyptic wasteland we all know and love in the near future.
